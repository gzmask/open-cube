Specs of the open cube

  • Each player computer acts as a server, which runs his player owned Cube (no central server required)
  • However the is one central server to store players' status and currencies
  • Each Cube room has 8 ports, connected to other player's owned Cube
  • There are two types of Cubes items: healing items and killing itmes
    • Healing items gives supplements
    • Healing items will only respawn for a defined period of time once consumed.
    • Killing items holds killing traps
    • kills in one player's owned Cube gains him currencies.
    • when player dies he will be returned to his owned cube with reduced status
  • Each Cube has a lock trigger. Only when this trigger been found, the 8 ports will be accessible
  • each port will only show how much the Cube worth(as how much the player spend on the items in this Cube)
